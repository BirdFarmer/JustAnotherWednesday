**The Wednesday Dilemma**

**The Premise:**
Next Wednesday, every person on Earth receives $1,000,000 deposited into their bank account.

**The Twist:**
You are one of a few special players who have a choice of when to receive your money:
- **Option A:** $500,000 this Monday (2 days early)
- **Option B:** $1,000,000 on Wednesday (with everyone else)
- **Option C:** $2,000,000 next Monday (5 days late)

Your choice, along with the global scenario, your personal role, location, and what you do with the money, determines your final PPU (Purchasing Power Units).
